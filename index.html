Exact Sciences Patient Assistance Program

We believe everyone should have access to the best healthcare options. Our patient assistance program helps patients facing challenges with insurance coverage receive their Cologuard screening at no cost. If you face these challenges and Cologuard is right for you, you may receive screening at no cost. Is your household at or below 400 percent of the Federal Poverty Level (FPL)? If so, you might be able to receive Cologuard screening at no cost.†‡
If you apply to receive no-cost Cologuard screening, you'll be asked to provide income documentation. If you qualify for other assistance programs, that may be enough to determine you are eligible for our program.§
Those programs include, but are not limited to:

  • Medicaid†
  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Home Energy Assistance Program

Have questions about our assistance program? Interested in applying? Please call our Customer Care Center at 1-844-870-8870 (select "billing option").

If you have any questions, Customer Care is available 24/7 at 1-844-870-8870

*Exact Sciences estimate based on historical patient billing. Rate of coverage varies by state and region. Exceptions for coverage may apply; only your patients' insurers can confirm how Cologuard would be covered.

†FPL is based on the thresholds for the 48 contiguous states and the District of Columbia

‡Some state programs assign a small copay for Cologuard

§Exact Sciences makes a determination of patient assistance in good faith based on an individualized assessment of financial need.
